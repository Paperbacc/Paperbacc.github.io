FAQ

Frequently asked questioins:

What is Paperbacc?


Paperbacc is an educational service where current International Baccalaureate students connect with alumni to receive feedback on IB papers. We offer reliable and effective feedback for current IB students and we offer alumni a chance to give feedback on IB papers as a good side-job.

I am an IB student, how does this work?


If you want to be a part of Paperbacc, go to the contact page and sign up as an IB student. We make an IB curriculum profile for you with the subjects you are currently taking. You submit your papers whenever you need feedback on them and we match you with the right IB alumnus.

I am an IB alumnus, how does this work?


If you want to be a part of Paperbacc, go to the contact page and sign up as an IB alumnus. We make a profile for you of your IB curriculum and the subjects that you excelled in and want to give feedback on. Match with the right IB student, review their papers and in exchange earn money!

Who are the alumni who will grade my papers?


We make sure that we connect you to an alumnus that has excelled in the respective IB subjects and feels comfortable in reviewing your paper. We also take into consideration what university and what degree the alumnus is currently enrolled in to ensure your papers are graded with quality and reliability.

When will I receive my paper back with the feedback?


Within 72 hours after your payment is processed.

How much is it going to cost to get feedback on my papers?


The price will differ depending on what type of essay you submit, and the length. The price range vary from 10 to 40 euros.
